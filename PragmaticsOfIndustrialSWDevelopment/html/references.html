Recommended Reading

  • Dean Wampler and Alex Payne, “Programming Scala”, O’Reilly, 2009. ISBN: 0596155957. You can read it for free by following the links on http://programmingscala.com or on Loyola’s Safari Books Online.
  • Harold Abelson and Gerald Jay Sussman, “Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs”, MIT Press, 1996. ISBN: 0262011530. Online access.
  • Andrew Hunt and David Thomas, “The Pragmatic Programmer: From Journeyman to Master”, Addison-Wesley, 1999. ISBN: 020161622X.

Recommended Reading: Scala and Git Resources

  • scala-lang.org: Downloads, tutorials, Scaladocs, etc.
  • Akka Framework and the documentation for it.
  • Pro Git. A well regarded book.
  • git-scm.com for downloads and documentation.
  • github.com (where the project code will be hosted) also has helpful documentation.
